Galactic foreground component-separation products

Low-resolution Galactic foreground products derived from Commander-based component-separation analyses of radio, microwave, and submillimetre data.

  • Synchrotron amplitude and spectral-index maps
  • Free-free emission maps
  • Anomalous microwave emission maps
  • Thermal dust and CMB products
  • Residual and uncertainty maps
