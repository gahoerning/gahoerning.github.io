Research

My research focuses on Galactic foreground emission, CMB component separation, and radio/millimetre-wave observations of the interstellar medium.

I work on modelling synchrotron, free-free, anomalous microwave emission, and thermal dust emission using multi-frequency data from radio, microwave, and submillimetre surveys.

Current projects

  • Low-frequency Galactic foreground modelling with Commander.
  • Polarised synchrotron modelling including Faraday rotation.
  • Free-free and AME studies in Galactic star-forming regions.
  • Machine-learning approaches for mitigating large-scale systematics in sky maps.
